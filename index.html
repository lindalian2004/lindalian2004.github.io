3. Kids' City Map that I designed

Urban studies/ Design/ Education
The highlight of the project is the originality of the children-friendly city map. There are very few well developed maps that are based on Iconic buildings for children in China, and even fewer of them have classifications of functional locations, like education locations, business locations, out door locations etc. Therefore, the long-term goal of this project is to create maps designed from the children's perspective to help them better understand our city. 

Project Achievement:
1. Two versions of the map were produced, one is in printed- version, and the other is an electronic map with detailed information.
2. Kid City Map were exhibited at the Beijing Urban Architecture Biennial in 2021 at the Beijing Urban Planning Institute;
3. We gave presentations to introduce our map to children in kindergartens affiliated with Beijing Jiaotong University.
